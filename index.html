We invest in businesses we understand, with consistent profits, good return on equity, competitive edge, capable and honest management, at a reasonable price.

For us, the future value of any investment arises from its present cost, thus, the higher the acquisition price, the lower the financial return. Therefore, we are interested in businesses whose acquisition price is attractive compared to its intrinsic value.
We pay special attention to company’s management and board members, monitoring their conduct through management decisions, disclosed results, and capital allocations. The company's reputation and its manager's credibility are crucial to our decision-making.

To conclude, we guide our conduct with ethical rigor, transparency, conviction, honesty, and commitment. We love what we do, and we intend to do it for as long as we are confident to be capable of giving a return better than a low-cost index fund.
